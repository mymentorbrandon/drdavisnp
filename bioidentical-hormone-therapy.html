Hormone therapy is not exclusively a women’s issue. Men experience testosterone decline — called andropause — that produces many of the same symptoms: fatigue, weight gain, brain fog, low drive, and emotional flatness. Both deserve restoration, not just medication to manage symptoms.

Dr. Laurie treats patients through all stages of hormonal change — perimenopause, menopause, post-menopause, andropause, and age-related decline. She also works with younger patients whose hormones are disrupted by stress, poor sleep, autoimmune conditions, or environmental factors.

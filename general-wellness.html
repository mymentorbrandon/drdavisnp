A Different Kind of Primary Care

Medicine focused on
keeping you well

The American healthcare system excels at crisis intervention. It was not designed for optimization. Most primary care visits are 15 minutes long, driven by insurance billing codes, and focused almost entirely on managing symptoms that already exist — not preventing the conditions that cause them.

“I want to know what your labs look like before something goes wrong — not after. Prevention is the most undervalued investment in medicine.” — Dr. Laurie

Dr. Laurie’s general wellness practice is built around a different standard: proactive, comprehensive, and personal. She spends real time with patients, interprets labs in clinical context, addresses root causes rather than symptoms, and creates a health plan that actually fits your life.

And she does it in your home — because that’s where she can see how you actually live, and where real care happens without the clinical noise.

What General Wellness Includes

Comprehensive care across
every dimension of health

Dr. Laurie’s general wellness practice is not a single service — it is a whole-person relationship. She evaluates, monitors, and actively manages your health across all the systems that determine how you feel, function, and age. Below are the core pillars of her wellness approach.

🧪

Comprehensive Lab Evaluation

Dr. Laurie orders and interprets a thorough panel — hormones, thyroid, metabolic markers, inflammation indicators, cardiovascular risk, nutritional levels, and more. She reads them in context — not just against a broad population range.

⚖️

Hormonal Health Monitoring

Hormonal decline begins earlier than most patients realize. Dr. Laurie tracks your hormone levels over time and intervenes with BHRT when appropriate — before symptoms become severe.

🧠

Cognitive & Mental Clarity

Brain fog, poor focus, and mood instability are clinical symptoms — not personality traits. Dr. Laurie evaluates neurological wellness through hormones, inflammation markers, thyroid function, and nutritional status.

🫀

Cardiovascular Risk Reduction

Beyond standard lipid panels, Dr. Laurie evaluates inflammatory markers, oxidative stress, and metabolic function that conventional cardiology often misses — and intervenes before disease develops.

🍽️

Nutrition & Gut Health

Gut health drives immune function, inflammation, mental health, and metabolic efficiency. Dr. Laurie provides practical, evidence-based nutritional guidance tailored to your labs and lifestyle.

💤

Sleep & Recovery Optimization

Poor sleep is not a personal failing — it is often a physiological issue driven by hormones, cortisol, thyroid function, or inflammation. Dr. Laurie identifies and addresses the root causes.

Family & Ongoing Care

Dr. Laurie’s general wellness practice serves individuals and families.

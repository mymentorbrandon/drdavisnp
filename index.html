Why Dr. Laurie Does What She Does

A mother’s conviction became a mission

Dr. Laurie didn’t arrive at regenerative medicine by accident. She was pushed there by something far more powerful than a career decision — her sons.

Her oldest son served as an infantry Marine in the United States Marine Corps, completing two tours of duty. The demands of that service — physically brutal and relentless — took a significant toll on his body. When he returned, Dr. Laurie watched the VA work through his injuries. What she saw troubled her deeply: a system too quick to recommend surgery, and providers too willing to reach for narcotics and steroid injections as the first and only answer to pain.

Her younger son was a competitive mixed martial artist — and with that came a different set of physical challenges, but the same frustrating pattern when he sought care. Quick diagnoses. Fast referrals to surgery. Prescriptions to numb the pain rather than find its source.

“I had been watching these clinics for years — handing out narcotics like they were the only tool in the box, or rushing patients straight to surgery without ever asking why. That’s not healing. That’s not medicine the way I believe it should be practiced.”

Dr. Laurie and her partners founded In Good Health in 2006 with a different vision — a family practice built around something most clinics had stopped offering: time, attention, and a genuine commitment to understanding what was actually happening in a patient’s body. The goal was never just to manage symptoms. It was to restore real function and real wellness.

Today, Dr. Laurie takes that mission a step further by bringing care directly into her patients’ homes across Tennessee and Georgia. From Chattanooga and across both states, she delivers private in-home consultations, regenerative therapies, and follow-up care — so her patients never have to compromise on the quality of their care again.

Regenerative Medicine

  • Accelerate healing of damaged tissue and cartilage

  • Improve energy, recovery, and quality of life

⚕ Stem cell therapy is not FDA-approved for all conditions. Results vary by individual. Dr. Laurie will provide a full clinical assessment and discuss whether this therapy is appropriate for your specific situation during your consultation.

What Are Mesenchymal Stem Cells?

MSCs are multipotent stromal cells derived from umbilical cord tissue — ethically obtained with full donor consent. They are valued for their ability to modulate inflammation, support tissue repair, and communicate with damaged cells.

Who Is a Good Candidate?

Individuals experiencing chronic pain, joint degeneration, autoimmune conditions, or seeking to improve general wellness and recovery may benefit. Dr. Laurie evaluates each patient individually before recommending any treatment.

How Is It Administered?

Therapy is delivered via IV infusion or targeted injection depending on the condition — performed in the privacy of your home by Dr. Laurie personally.

Is It Safe?

Umbilical cord MSC therapy has an established safety profile. Dr. Laurie uses only screened, properly processed biologics from accredited labs. Full informed consent is obtained prior to any procedure.

Common Questions

Answers from Dr. Laurie

Is stem cell therapy legal and safe?

Yes. Umbilical cord mesenchymal stem cell therapy is legal in the United States when administered by a licensed practitioner using properly processed, FDA-registered biologics from accredited labs. Dr. Laurie uses only fully vetted products and obtains informed consent from every patient.

Does insurance cover these services?

Most regenerative and alternative wellness services are not covered by traditional insurance. Dr. Laurie’s practice operates as a private-pay model, allowing her to focus entirely on your care without insurance restrictions. Transparent pricing is discussed during your consultation.

How far does Dr. Laurie travel?

Dr. Laurie is based in Chattanooga, TN and provides private in-home care throughout Tennessee and Georgia. Coverage within each state is discussed during intake. Reach out at (423) 867-4969 to confirm your area.

What is bioidentical hormone therapy?

Bioidentical hormones are compounds identical in structure to those your body produces naturally. Unlike synthetic hormones, BHRT is customized based on your lab results and symptoms. It can address fatigue, weight gain, mood changes, brain fog, low libido, and more — naturally.

What happens at the first visit?

Your first visit is a private online consultation via Google Meet. Dr. Laurie reviews your health history, current symptoms, medications, and goals in detail. She may order labs following the call. There is no rush — this conversation is the foundation of everything that follows.

Is this a replacement for my regular doctor?

Dr. Laurie provides complementary, integrative, and preventive care. She encourages patients to maintain relationships with their existing physicians and is happy to coordinate with your care team when appropriate.

The Problem With Conventional Pain Care

You’ve been managing.
Not healing.

The conventional pain management model in America is built around suppression. Cortisone shots to reduce inflammation for weeks. Opioids to block pain signals. Muscle relaxants. Sleep aids. All of them temporarily reducing your experience of pain — while the underlying cause continues unchecked.

“I watched the VA give my son shot after shot and prescription after prescription. He wasn’t getting better — he was getting dependent. That’s what drove me to build something different.” — Dr. Laurie

The result of this approach: diminishing returns on injections, rising medication doses, progressive tissue damage, and eventual surgery — often delivering the same temporary relief at far greater cost and risk.

Dr. Laurie was trained in conventional medicine and has spent decades in clinical practice. She knows precisely what the standard protocol looks like — and precisely why it fails so many patients over time.
